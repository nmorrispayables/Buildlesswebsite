The problem

Your ERP data is rich.
Your decisions are slow.

Manufacturing teams spend hours every week running reports, copying data between screens, and hunting for exceptions that should have been flagged the moment they happened.

⏱

Exceptions discovered too late

Stockouts, BOM gaps, expired lots, and scrap spikes surface hours or days after they happen — costing real money in carrying costs and emergency expedites.

⛌

Every report is a project

Workflow improvements require custom reports, a SQL developer, or a $250K consulting engagement. Most teams just live with the gaps.

⌒

Skilled labor wasted on retrieval

Your most experienced operators spend hours every day pulling data instead of making decisions. Labor shortages make this unsustainable.

↯

Decisions are reactive, not proactive

By the time the right person sees the right number, the moment to act has passed. ERP becomes a record of what happened — not a tool to shape what's next.

The agents

From quote to cash.
And everything in between.

AI agents that run your operations alongside Plex — from quote to cash, and unlimited custom agents in between.

SALES

Quoting Agent

Reads CRM signals, historical pricing, and margin targets to recommend a quote — in seconds. No more gut-feel pricing or waiting on a sales engineer.

"What should we quote for this RFQ?"
RECEIVING

Order Entry

Parses customer PO PDFs and pushes a structured sales order into Plex — ready for review and approval in seconds, not hours.

"Process the 14 POs that came in overnight."
PRODUCTION

Scheduling Agent

Sequences work orders across the floor based on due dates, machine availability, and capacity constraints. Keeps jobs moving without the manual shuffle.

"Sequence the floor for this week."
INVENTORY

MRP Agent

Reads open demand and on-hand inventory. Recommends purchase actions for non-EDI material, with a clear view of what to order and when.

"What raw materials do I need to order?"
FINANCE

Job Cost Analyzer

Monitors job costs in real time and flags variance before it compounds. Surfaces bottlenecks and overruns the moment they appear on the floor.

"Which jobs are over budget right now?"
ACCOUNTS RECEIVABLE

Deposit Automation

Matches incoming remittances to open AR invoices in Plex. Closes the loop from shipment to cash with no manual reconciliation.

"Apply today's deposits to open invoices."

How it works

Connects to Plex.
Acts on your behalf.

No rip-and-replace. No custom development. BuildLess connects to your existing Plex environment through the Plex Connect API.

01 / CONNECT

Plug into Plex

Connect to your Plex tenant in minutes using the Plex Connect API. Your data stays in Plex — BuildLess reads and acts through standard, audited endpoints.

02 / DEPLOY

Activate agents

Choose from pre-built agents for inventory, BOM, receiving, shipping, and quality. Or build your own for the workflows that matter most to your floor.

03 / RUN

Agents run on demand or on a schedule. They surface exceptions, draft actions, and wait for your approval. You stay in control. They do the heavy lifting.
